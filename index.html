About MC Media

MC Media was founded with a simple mission: to ensure creators get paid for their work. In today's digital landscape, music travels further and faster than ever before, but often without the proper attribution and compensation for the artists who created it.

Our innovative approach embeds copyright information directly into music files, creating a permanent record of ownership that travels with the work. This serves as both legal precedent and a foundation for integration with audio tracking technologies.

As we continue to grow, we're expanding our services to include copyright management consulting and software customization for large-scale music catalog management, specifically targeting producers and publishers.

We believe in a future where creativity is properly valued and compensated, and we're building the tools to make that future a reality.
