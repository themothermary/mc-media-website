MC Media is an early-stage startup dedicated to helping creators secure ownership of their work. We provide easy-to-use tools that embed copyright information directly into music files. This ensures that wherever your music travels digitally, your ownership and legal data travel with it—acting as both legal precedent and a foundation for integration with audio tracking technologies. As we grow into a larger platform, we plan to expand our services to include copyright management consulting and software customization for large-scale music catalog management, specifically targeting producers and publishers. We believe creators deserve to get paid for what they make, and our mission is to make that possible—simply and efficiently.

what we offer

MC Media is committed to making digital and media asset management more accessible and effective. Our mission is to empower creators and producers with top-notch support, innovative tools, and efficient processes to enhance their product management. We address the pressing need to improve copyright management in digital products, ensuring fair and transparent practices. From production planning to audiovisual catalog management, categorization, and archiving, we aim to elevate copyright awareness and promote fairness in the creative industries. By streamlining workflows and providing cutting-edge solutions, we ensure that creators can focus on what they do best—creating—while we handle the complexities of asset management.

about MC Media

MC Media emerged from the growing need for better copyright protection and media organization in the creative industries. We believe in transparency, innovation, and fairness as the foundation of digital asset management. Our team combines deep industry expertise with cutting-edge technology to create solutions that empower creators and protect their intellectual property. From our headquarters, we serve artists, producers, and media companies worldwide, helping them navigate the complex landscape of digital rights management with confidence and clarity.
