About MC Media

MC Media was founded on the principle that creators deserve to maintain ownership and get paid for their work. In today's digital landscape, music can travel across countless platforms and formats, often losing connection to its original creator along the way.

Our solution embeds copyright information directly into music files themselves, creating an indelible link between creator and creation. This technology serves both as legal precedent for ownership claims and as infrastructure for future audio tracking systems.

As we grow, we're expanding our services to include comprehensive rights management consulting and customized software solutions for large-scale music catalog management. We're committed to building tools that put power back in the hands of creators.

Duck1 Testing Phase for Music Rights Management

Our latest project, Duck1, has entered its testing phase. This innovative approach to music rights management uses embedded copyright technology to ensure creators maintain ownership of their work across digital platforms.

The testing phase will involve a select group of creators who will use our technology to protect their music. We'll be gathering feedback and making improvements before a wider release.

Music Rights Clinic Launch

We're excited to announce the launch of our Music Rights Clinic, a resource for creators to get answers to their copyright questions and understand their rights.

The clinic will offer free consultations, educational materials, and guidance on navigating the complex world of music copyright. Our goal is to empower creators with knowledge about protecting their work.
